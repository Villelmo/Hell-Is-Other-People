Hell Is Other People is an early attempt at what I'm calling anti-social media. By connecting your FourSquare account, this site will automatically track your check-ins and help me to stay as far from you as possible.

Authorize

No personally identifying information is stored—just the time and geographical location of your last check-in along with an encrypted id so I can tell if you're a new user or somebody that's updating. I don't do anything that would affect your FourSquare account in any way. I don't even save the access token they give me to allow that.
